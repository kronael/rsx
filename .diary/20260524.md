## 13:44

Three real CMP reliability bugs surfaced during the
narrative-correction discussion, all specced + deferred to
implement:
1. Silent reorder_buf overflow at 512 entries — receiver
   silently advances past lost seqs, violates FIFO. Should
   surface FAULTED state and force consumer to DXS-replay.
2. NAK storm — every out-of-order packet fires a fresh NAK
   covering the whole gap, plus every 10 ms heartbeat re-
   fires unconditionally. O(N²) retransmits under sustained
   loss. Need per-gap receiver-side debounce.
3. No sender-side retransmit dedup — overlapping NAKs cause
   same seq retransmitted N times. Need per-seq rate limit
   (skip if retransmitted within last RTT).
Spec at .ship/23-CMP-RELIABILITY-FIXES/SPEC.md.
